About Us

You can count on the undivided attention of a team that prizes relationships over profits

A Law Firm with more than 50 Years in Combined Experience...

S. Efejuku & Co. Chambers is a leading team of attorneys, barristers and solicitors specialising in commercial and financial litigation, arbitration aw well as public law. As consistently identified in the legal directories of the NBA and in the eyes of many SANs, the members of S. Efejuku & Co. Chambers remain the top choice for critical, large-scale commercial litigation and arbitration.

Stephen Efejuku [Esq], the founder of S. Efejuku & Co. Chambers is dual qualified as an Attorney and Solicitor. Stephen completed his legal studies in Uniben to bag the LLB degree and proceeded to Law School to bag the BL certificate.

Over the years Stephen has gained extensive experience in Real Estate and Property Law, National Law, Contract Law, Succession Law, and Taxation.
