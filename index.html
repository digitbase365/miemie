About Us

10 Years Of Experience In Legal Solutions

S. EFEJUKU & CO is a fully serviced law firm established and headed by Stephen Efejuku, Esq., an active member of the Warri Branch of the Nigeria Bar Association. Providing stellar legal counsel and representation to Companies, Businesses as well as Oil and Gas Communities for over 10 years.

When we set up Miemie Chambers, we wanted to be our clients’ guiding light and have them think of us as trusted, practical and attentive. Ten years down the line, we’re still acting as our clients’ guiding light and we remain true to those values. Our vision was – and remains – to create a disruptive, innovative and lean (low-overhead, technology-driven) law firm in West Africa to challenge the status quo.
